## Description

The **Oracle Cloud Infrastructure 2025 Certified AI Foundations Associate** certification validates foundational knowledge of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning concepts with a focus on Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI) services and solutions.

The certification introduces core AI and ML principles, including supervised and unsupervised learning, deep learning architectures, generative AI, and Large Language Models (LLMs). It also provides practical insights into Oracle's AI ecosystem and how OCI AI services can be used to build intelligent, cloud-based business solutions.

This certification serves as a strong foundation for professionals seeking to understand modern AI technologies and their implementation within enterprise cloud environments.
